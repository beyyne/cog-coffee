About Us

Welcome to the Cognition Coffee Bar — our in-office cafe for the team. Order ahead, grab your drink, and get back to building.

We are intentional about every detail. Our whole milk is 100% Jersey cow from Valley Ford Creamery in Sonoma County. Our matcha is Japanese matcha sourced from Spirit Tea in Chicago. Our pour over program rotates an international, local, and domestic roaster each month — we like to explore some of the best coffee roasters down to some of the best dairy farms in California.

Order from your desk, pick up at the bar. Simple as that.
